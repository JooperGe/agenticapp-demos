# Viral Growth Playbook

## 1. Working Product

- Ship a polished MVP with one clear "aha moment"
- Zero-friction onboarding (guest mode, no forced sign-up)
- Mobile-first, fast load, no broken flows
- Core loop must work end-to-end before any marketing

## 2. Sharp Distribution Strategy (WeChat / Social)

- **WeChat Mini Program** — default gateway for Chinese users
- **Share cards** with preview image + one-line hook on every share action
- **Social templates** — pre-filled share text, branded images, QR codes
- **KOL seeding** — identify 10-20 micro-influencers in the niche, give early access
- **Community groups** — WeChat groups, Xiaohongshu posts, Douyin short videos
- **SEO/ASO** — keyword-optimize the app store listing and landing page

## 3. Organic Growth Hook

- **Invite rewards** — unlock feature/content for each successful invite (not just sign-up)
- **Content virality** — generated results are shareable by design (score card, report, image, link)
- **Social proof loop** — show "X friends are using this" or live activity feed
- **Network effects** — product gets better as more people use it (collaboration, rankings, data)
- **Referral mechanics** — track referral source, attribute referrals, reward both sides

## Execution Order

1. Lock the core product loop (days)
2. Bake in shareability & growth hooks (days)
3. Prepare share assets + seeding content (days)
4. Soft launch to seed users → iterate on conversion
5. Push to broader channels based on what converts
